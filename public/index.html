What is the actual interview process at Nexora Tech for PM roles — and what do they really look for?
Verified insider 2 days ago You have access
SC
Sarah Chen  · Sr. PM at Nexora 3 days ago
4 rounds. Intro call (30 min, culture fit), take-home PRD case (say 2h, budget 3h), panel with 3 PMs + 1 engineer, final with CPO. They care deeply about how you handle ambiguity — show how you'd actually think, not how you've learned to perform thinking.

Daniel Lowe  · PM at Nexora (3 yrs) 2 days ago
The take-home is brutal but fair. They give you a real problem they're solving (disguised). My case: redesign onboarding for enterprise customers with 50+ seat licenses. They want specificity, not theory.
